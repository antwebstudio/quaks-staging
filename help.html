If you have received an incorrect or factory defective item(s), you may be eligible for a return, refund, or exchange of item(s) at our sole discretion.

To make a claim please contact us at quackscollectible@outlook.com within TEN (10) days of your receipt of the item. Due to the limited nature of our collectibles, we may face problems in resolving your issue if you contact us any later.
To process your claim, we will need the following details of the allegedly incorrect, damaged, or defective item(s):
(a) Please explain why the item(s) delivered is incorrect, or what is the damage/defect in the item(s);
(b) A photo of the incorrect item(s), or the alleged damage/defect to the item(s); and
(c) A photo of the outer box with the shipping label intact.
To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and shipped back to us in its original condition with its original tags and/or packaging intact. Note also that customers may be liable for shipping charges, duties and taxes for returns, and that such charges are non-refundable.
We will process your claim and respond to you via email within five (5) business days upon receipt of your claim. Once your item is received by us, we will start the refund process immediately. Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank/original method of payment.

Please ensure that you have provided us with the right address prior to committing to your order. A refused shipment will be treated as a return, for which you may be liable to pay for any reshipment or disposal costs, or to have such amount deducted from any refunds, at our sole discretion. Any free shipment extended by us is limited to the initial shipment of the item. Quacks Collectible shall not be liable for any subsequent shipment charges due to wrong information provided by the customer or for any re-delivery thereafter if the customer is unavailable to accept delivery on the first shipment.

Please note that this Return, Refund & Exchange Policy is only applicable to items which you have purchased directly from us through our online store. If you have any issues with a Quacks Collectible product that you did not purchase directly from us, please contact the retailer directly that you purchased it from. Kindly note that we do not accept any exchanges/returns for customers who have purchased a Quacks Collectible item from other retailers. Please contact the respective retail store directly for such exchange/return requests.

Unfortunately, we are not able to cancel orders once they have been confirmed. Please only place an order (or pre-order) when you know you absolutely want the item(s). We will not be able to cancel your order should you change your mind.
Do note that our collectibles are often listed on our website on a pre-order basis. This means that the item that you purchase may not have been manufactured yet. While we strive to have our items delivered to you in a timely fashion, our collectibles are often hand-painted and assembled, and may take several months from the close of the pre-order to reach you.
